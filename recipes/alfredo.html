chicken alfredo pasta recipe

description

creamy and juicy chicken alfredo pasta

recipe straight from italy

Ingredients

  • 8 oz fettuccine
  • 1 lb chicken breast, sliced
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan
  • Salt, pepper, garlic (optional)

steps

  1. Cook pasta.
  2. Season and cook chicken in a pan until golden; set aside.
  3. In the same pan, melt butter, add cream, then Parmesan; stir until smooth.
  4. Add chicken and pasta to the sauce; toss to coat.
  5. Serve warm!
