pink lemonade recipe

despription

pink lemonade is a delicious beverage made of mixing both lemons and starwberies

and this is a very easy recipe for it to make

ingredients

  • 3 lemons
  • starwberies
  • water
  • ice
  • 1/2 cup of sugar

steps

  1. Stir lemon juice and sugar until the sugar dissolves.
  2. Add cold water and mix well.
  3. Serve over ice and enjoy!
