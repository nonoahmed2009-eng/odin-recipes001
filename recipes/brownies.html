brownies recipe

description

this is the ultimate brownies recipe easy to make and delicious

Ingredients

  • ½ cup (115 g) butter, melted
  • 1 cup (200 g) sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • ⅓ cup (35 g) unsweetened cocoa powder
  • ½ cup (65 g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

    • steps

    1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease an 8×8 inch pan.
    2. In a bowl, mix melted butter, sugar, eggs, and vanilla.
    3. Add cocoa, flour, and salt; stir until just combined.
    4. Pour into the pan and smooth the top.
    5. Bake 20–25 minutes, until the center is set but still fudgy.
    6. Cool, slice, enjoy!
